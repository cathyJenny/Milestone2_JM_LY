Hobbit: The Battle of the Five Armies

Release Date

Dec 17, 2014

Genres:

Action/Adventure

Director

Peter Jackson

Cast:

Martin Freeman, Ian McKellen, Richard Armitage, Andy Serkis, Elijah Wood

Description

Having reclaimed Erebor and a vast treasure from the dragon Smaug, Thorin Oakenshield (Richard Armitage) sacrifices friendship and honor to horde the treasure, despite Smaug's fiery wrath and Bilbo's (Martin Freeman) desperate attempts to make him see reason. Meanwhile, Sauron sends forth legions of Orcs in a sneak attack upon the Lonely Mountain. As the ultimate fate of Middle Earth hangs in the balance, the races of Men, Elves and Dwarves must decide whether to unite or die.
